Key Platform Capabilities

Everything you need for efficient loan management.

Online Loan Applications

Users can submit loan applications directly through the system with a simple form. All application data is securely stored and accessible to administrators for review.

Admin Control Dashboard

Administrators can view loan applications, approve or reject requests, and track the entire system through a centralized dashboard.

EMI Calculator

Calculate monthly EMI before applying for a loan. Get instant calculations with detailed breakdowns of interest and total payments.

Loan Status Tracking

Users can check whether their loan is pending, approved, or rejected. Real-time updates on application progress.

Payment Monitoring

Track EMI payments and repayment schedules. Automated reminders and payment confirmations.

Defaulter Identification

Automatically detect borrowers who miss payments. Proactive alerts and management tools.
